About the Project

This is a Temperature Converter app that allows users to convert temperatures between Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin, and Reaumur.

Conversion Formulas

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: (°F − 32) × 5/9
  • Celsius to Kelvin: °C + 273.15
  • Kelvin to Celsius: K − 273.15
  • Reaumur to Celsius: °Ré × 1.25
